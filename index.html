Mindfulness

Mindfulness is the basic human ability to be fully present, aware of where we are and what we are doing and not overlay reactive or overwhelmed by what is going on around us. While mindfulness is something we all naturally possess, it is more readily available to us when we practice daily. The goal of mindfulness is to wake up to the inner workings of our mental, emotional, and physical processes.
